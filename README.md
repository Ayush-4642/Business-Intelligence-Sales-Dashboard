# Business Intelligence & Sales Analytics Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

The **Business Intelligence & Sales Analytics Dashboard** is an interactive Power BI solution designed to analyze sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, and regional trends. The dashboard transforms raw sales data into actionable business insights that support data-driven decision-making.

This project demonstrates the use of **Business Intelligence (BI)** concepts, data visualization, KPI tracking, and sales analytics to evaluate overall business performance.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

- Monitor overall sales and profit performance.
- Identify top-performing product categories.
- Analyze regional sales distribution.
- Track monthly sales trends.
- Identify high-value customers.
- Support strategic business decision-making through data insights.

# 📈 Business Questions Answered

## 1. How is the business performing overall?

### Dashboard Visuals
- KPI Cards
  - Total Sales
  - Total Profit
  - Total Orders
  - Total Quantity Sold

### Business Value
Provides management with a quick overview of business performance and operational health.

### Insight
The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor revenue generation, profitability, and order volume from a single view.

---

## 2. Which product categories generate the highest sales and profit?

### Dashboard Visuals
- Sales by Category
- Profit by Category

### Business Value
Helps identify the most successful product categories and areas where investment can generate higher returns.

### Insight
Management can prioritize high-performing categories and optimize inventory planning.

## 3. Which regions contribute the most revenue?

### Dashboard Visuals
- State-wise Sales Analysis
- Regional Performance Charts
